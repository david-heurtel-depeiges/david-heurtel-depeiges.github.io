## Preprints, submitted papers and work in progress

| Title | Authors and short summary | Link |
| --- | --- | --- |
|Removing Dust from CMB Observations with Diffusion Model| In this paper with [Blakesley Burkhart](https://www.bburkhart.com/), [Ruben Ohana](https://rubenohana.github.io/) and [Bruno Regaldo-Saint Blancard](https://users.flatironinstitute.org/~bregaldosaintblancard/), we present a proof of concept for refining the modeling of the Galactic dust foreground, a pivotal aspect of cosmic microwave background (CMB) observations. Employing diffusion models trained on simulated dust emission maps, we demonstrate their potential in component separation of mixed signals of dust and CMB. We show that key summary statistics of said fields are well recovered by this process, paving the way for cosmological inference.| [arXiv]() |
